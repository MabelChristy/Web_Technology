Grammy Award for Best Pop Vocal Album

In 2019, Ariana Grande's undeniable talent and magnetic presence propelled her to victory at the MTV Video Music Awards, where she was crowned Artist of the Year. Grande's chart-topping hits, captivating performances, and unwavering dedication to her craft solidified her as a dominant force in the music industry. With this prestigious accolade, Grande's influence and impact on pop culture were celebrated, marking yet another milestone in her illustrious career.
